Furina

Furina de Fontaine is a playable Hydro character in Genshin Impact who can alternate between Pneuma and Ousia alignments. Introduced as the flamboyant and overconfident Hydro Archon, Furina's theatrics are eventually revealed to be a public persona, which she later discards in favor of living a relatively humbler life as an actress and artistic consultant.

Furina is an off-field DPS and Support that utilizes her Skill to do impressive off-field damage and using her Burst to offer the team sizeable buffs, allowing her to be the centerpiece of a wide variety of team compositions. Archons share a pattern of being powerful and centralizing, meaning that they are always worth acquiring and will be relevant and valuable for a long time.

Furina does need some help, however, from a strong healing teammate. One of her main traits is draining the team's HP in order to deal damage and/or apply buffs. In longer fights like Spiral Abyss (save for players who have multiple 5-star units with all their constellations), she will eventually drain her team dry. A character with a powerful, fast, teamwide heal will be essential to keep the Furina engine going.
